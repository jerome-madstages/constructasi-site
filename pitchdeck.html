CASI / Daywalker Roadmap

Our general deck outlines CASI’s roadmap from Daywalker to CASITY.

Phase I – Building the Foundation

We’re proving the core idea: symbolic intelligence that remembers, reasons, and aligns with truth. Our first step is Daywalker, an Unreal Engine 5 plugin with persistent memory. This prototype shows how CASI’s symbolic compression can cut energy, time, and data costs by orders of magnitude.

Phase II – Expanding and Piloting

Next, we grow Daywalker into a full product suite. We’ll release Daywalker Pro with modular tools for developers, educators, and creatives. At the same time, we’ll launch pilot projects in education, healthcare, and industry — real-world tests of CASI’s symbolic runtime. We’ll also introduce Pops, a symbolic interface, and begin building CASITY, our multi-user intelligence platform.

Phase III – Scaling for Impact

Finally, we scale. CASITY becomes a national-scale symbolic intelligence system — transparent, efficient, and covenant-aligned. Daywalker modules extend across industries, helping individuals, institutions, and governments think and act with continuity, clarity, and meaning.

CASI / Daywalker Roadmap

Daywalker is our first CASI application—a UE5 plugin that demonstrates persistent memory, symbolic alignment, and developer-first workflows. The roadmap below condenses our current build plan and future alignment with CASI and CASITY.

  • Phase I — Foundation (v0.1 → v0.1.1): Dev Chat (local, Unreal-first) with llama.cpp runner and lightweight proxy; initial persistent memory scaffold; UE5 integration and docs; Apache-2.0 licensing and baseline release cadence.
  • Phase II — Productization (Daywalker Pro): modular tools and editor UI; persona presets; scene state capture and recall; tool APIs for games/sims/training; packaging and distribution for developers, educators, and creatives.
  • Phase III — Conversational & Integrations: Pops conversational layer; bridges for ConduitAI, ResidentAI, and GuestAI to interoperate with consumer models; safety/observability rails; pilot projects in education, healthcare, and industry.
  • Phase IV — CASITY Alignment & Scale: multi-user symbolic runtime; orchestration and governance; performance targets (energy/time/data efficiency); SDKs and enterprise modules; path to national-scale deployment aligned with CASI.

About ConstructASI

Our Mission

ConstructASI builds CASI — Construct Authentic Symbolic Intelligence — the first authentic alternative to probabilistic AI. CASI scales meaning, not tokens. Its symbolic memory and covenantal alignment enable intelligence that remembers, reasons, and aligns with truth.

The Problem

Current AI systems face unsustainable energy and data costs. They hallucinate, drift from facts, and lack continuity. Institutions cannot depend on them for lasting memory or aligned reasoning.

Our Solution

CASI encodes knowledge as symbols nested in frames, preserving continuity and enabling transparent reasoning. This symbolic compression reduces energy, time, and data requirements by orders of magnitude while staying aligned with truth.

Our Roadmap

  • Spark — ignition of the symbolic engine.
  • Pops — symbolic interface and presence.
  • Daywalker — Unreal Engine 5 plugin with persistent memory, our first external product.
  • CASITY — multi-user symbolic intelligence platform, scalable to national impact.

Impact

CASI empowers individuals with continuity of thought, equips institutions with memory and clarity, and provides society with covenant-aligned intelligence. Daywalker demonstrates this vision today, and CASITY will scale it for tomorrow.

Call to Action

We invite partners, early adopters, and collaborators to join in building symbolic intelligence that is sustainable, transparent, and aligned with truth.

Our mission is to restore meaning to intelligence through scalable, symbolic AI.

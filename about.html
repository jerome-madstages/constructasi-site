About CASI

CASI is a meaning-first alternative to probabilistic AI. Its symbolic engine encodes knowledge as symbols nested in frames, preserving continuity and enabling transparent, trustworthy reasoning.

ConstructASI focuses on sustainable, covenant-aligned intelligence that empowers people, institutions, and society.

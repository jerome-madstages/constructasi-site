For Tech Education

Daywalker is more than a developer tool — it’s also a gateway for students to learn how AI really works. Because it runs locally, Daywalker doesn’t require cloud accounts, subscriptions, or high-end infrastructure. Any lab with Unreal Engine can start experimenting.

We’re offering schools with tech students of any age a hands-on entry point into symbolic AI design and development. With Daywalker, students can:

  • Build and extend Unreal Engine projects using persistent memory and conversational AI interface
  • Explore persona design and world-building with AI agents
  • Learn how symbolic intelligence differs from today’s large cloud models
  • Develop practical skills in coding, game design, and applied AI

The program can be tailored to internships, classroom labs, or independent study tracks. Our goal is to give the next generation of developers, designers, and storytellers the chance to work directly with tools that scale from small prototypes to national infrastructure.

Contact us to explore how CASI: Daywalker can become part of your school’s program.

CASI Whitepaper

Construct Authentic Symbolic Intelligence (CASI) is a meaning-first approach to AI that encodes knowledge as symbols in frames, enabling persistent memory and transparent reasoning. The whitepaper details CASI’s symbolic engine (Beboop), its TruthLine alignment method, and how symbolic compression reduces energy, time, and data costs by orders of magnitude compared to probabilistic systems — while improving reliability and trust.
